Real-Time Emotion Detection and Mental Health Support System

Real-Time Emotion Recognition for Personalized Mental Health Support

Our system integrates facial expression analysis, eye blink monitoring, and breathing pattern detection to continuously assess users' emotional states. By processing real-time data through an ensemble model, it delivers tailored interventions via an AI-powered chatbot.


Key Features: Our solution combines multiple inputs and cutting‚Äêedge algorithms to provide effective mental health support:

  • Multi-Modal Emotion Recognition. Utilizes facial expression analysis, eye blink rate monitoring, and breathing pattern detection to assess emotional states.
  • Ensemble Data Fusion. Combines insights from multiple inputs using advanced ensemble models for accurate emotion prediction.
  • Real-Time Processing. Continuously analyzes camera input to provide near-instantaneous emotional insights.
  • AI-Powered Chatbot Support. Engages users with personalized mental health advice, relaxation techniques, and resource recommendations based on their emotional state.
  • Adaptive Feedback Loop. Continuously learns from user feedback to improve emotion detection accuracy and refine chatbot responses.
  • Comprehensive Visualization. Offers dashboards and trend reports to help users track their emotional patterns over time.

Technologies & Challenges: Our solution leverages computer vision libraries like OpenCV and Dlib, pre-trained deep learning models (FER-2013, AffectNet), transformer-based NLP models (such as GPT and Hugging Face), and Scikit-learn for ensemble modeling. Key challenges include ensuring real-time performance, safeguarding sensitive emotional data, and adapting to individual variances in non-verbal cues.

Future Enhancements

Plans include integrating wearable devices for more precise physiological monitoring, expanding the chatbot with voice interaction capabilities, and partnering with mental health professionals for a comprehensive support system. This innovative approach aims to create a positive social impact by enabling early intervention and personalized care.
